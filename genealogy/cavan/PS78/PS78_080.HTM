Notes for Henry SCHOLTE
Henry and Elizabeth lived in Hepworth, Ontario.196

"The lineage of Henry and Elizabeth is EXTENSIVE! and almost entirely lives in the USA, though two of their children (besides Norman), John and Val, lived in Winnepeg. After Elizabeth's death, Henry married their housekeeper and moved to Emo, Ont., and then to Indus, MN."196
Research Notes
NAME: His last name is also given as SCHOLTA.208

MARRIAGE: There is a great deal of confusion in the information for this family. Everyone agrees that Henry SCHOLTE married Elizabeth McKAGUE, but there is a difference of opinion as to which Elizabeth McKAGUE it was. I believe the correct one is as shown--the daughter of John McKAGUE and Nancy McINDOO.196 The alternative is the daughter of G. Wilson McKAGUE and Nancy Ann McINDOO.54 Other sources indicate that the latter Elizabeth was married to Henry's brother Frederick,2 12 50 9 and for the moment I am making that assignment.
