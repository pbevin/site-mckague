Notes for Mary Elizabeth McMASTER
Cobourg Sentinel-Star, Thursday, December 1, 1955:
"Obituary
"MRS. JAMES McKAGUE
"Mrs. James McKague passed away at Port Hope Hospital, November 11, at the age of 86 years, after an illness of some time.
"The former Mary Elizabeth McMaster, daughter of Alex and Jane McMaster, was born at Port Hope and had married James McKague who predeceased her twenty years ago.
"One son, Eric, survives her, also a sister, Mrs. Will Oke of Port Hope. Two sisters predeceased her, Mrs. (Anna) Long, Port Hope, and Mrs. (Eleanor) Hipwell, Toronto.
"The funeral took place from the George Funeral Home, Port Hope, with burial at Baltimore United Church cemetery. Rev. B.K. Cronk and Rev. A.C. Luffman officiated. The pallbearers were Allan Oke, Oshawa; Hugh Coleman, Dr. D. Walden, Port Hope, Bev. Hancock, Port Hope, Frank Clapperton, Baltimore."128
