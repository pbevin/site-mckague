Notes for Hiram McKAGUE
Hiram moved from Mariposa Township to Drayton ON.6 In 1882, Hiram and his family moved to R.M. Woodworth (originally part of County of Dennis).193 He was granted land at NW-32-10-25-W1 [prefaced by PT--does this mean part?].210

The first picnic ever held at the Manitou Lake Picnic grounds was on Dominion Day, July 1, 1905, and Hiram was one of a very small party of men who searched the area for a good location.205
