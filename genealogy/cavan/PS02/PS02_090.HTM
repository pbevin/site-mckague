Notes for Jane McKAGUE
One source2 shows only an unknown daughter of John McKague and Jane Wilson, born in Ireland, with no other information. The source giving name and birthdate290 does not identify parents. The identification of Jane as this unknown daughter is tentative at best. The dates and places coincide nicely with where other family members were, and there are other connections (e.g., Jane's daughter Jane appears to have married a man whose siblings married children of Jane's brother George).

Jane was enumerated in the 1861 provincial census in Ontario and in the 1871 federal census in Wellington County, Ontario.290
