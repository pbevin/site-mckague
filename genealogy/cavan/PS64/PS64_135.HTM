Notes for Charles Lewis BISSELL
"Charles came to Weaton, Illinois with his parents in 1874. After he married in 1877, Charles and Emily moved to Chicago, Illinois for 4 years, then to Grand Haven, Michigan for a year and a half. Then to Plainfield Twnship In Kent Co, Michigan, where they had the Bissell Fruit Farm for 11 years. About 1895, they moved to Grand Rapids, Michigan, where they resided at 25 Benjamin St. SE."395
