Notes for Harriet Ruth Grace WILSON
When Grayce became an American citizen, her DOB of 1919 was mistakenly entered as 1929. Elroy always teased her about being his "child bride". All subsequent documentation kept her 10 years younger and she even waited an extra 10 years for her pension so she could stay young.153
Research Notes
NAME: Her first name(s) is/are also given as Grace5 and as Grayce. She had already adopted the spelling "Grayce" in 1944, when she signed her name as "Grayce Massey".154 As of 2001, she was using the name Grayce Ruth.153
