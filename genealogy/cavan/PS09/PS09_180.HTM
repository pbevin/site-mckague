Notes for John Robert Galbraith MACPHERSON
John and Etta lived on the Massey homestead. They moved to Cobourg in 1900 just before Etta's death. John's housekeeper was Annie Louisa Marietta Massey (Gram). John moved to North Dakota in the fall of 1906, leaving Harry and Jennie (and maybe Bruce) behind. They spent the winter with an aunt and uncle (probably Marcus and Mary Anne) and Harry and Jennie (and maybe Bruce) got their own place in the spring.113
