Notes for Eleanor M. KELLEY
In 1870, Eleanor was living in Portland Twp, Cerro Gordo Co. IA.434 In 1875, she was in LaCrosse Twp, Jackson Co. MN.435 In 1880, she was living in LaCrosse Twp, Jackson Co. MN with her sister Ethelina and family as a servant.619

By 1900, Eleanor and Nels had moved north to Parks Rapids, Hubbard Co. MN. By about 1905, they had moved west to the Marcus, Stevens Co. WA area along the Columbia River. After Nels died in 1922, Eleanor moved onto the west coast of Washington to lived with her son Ira O. Peterson and his family.606
