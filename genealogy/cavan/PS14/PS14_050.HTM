Notes for Richard KELLEY
The KELLEY family left Ireland and went to Vermont. At that point the spelling was "Kelley".401

The Ontario farm (now owned by Bud and Molly LECKIE) received its patent on Sep 15, 1803. It was purchased on Dec 22,1846 by James D. GOSLEE. It was purchased on Dec 27, 1869 from Richard ALGER by Richard KELLEY. There is no record of a transfer of title from GOSLEE to ALGER but ALGER may have been an executor of an estate.401

In 1871, Richard and Anne were living in Cramahe Township with their children Mary, John, George, William, James and Charles.160

In 1891, they were still in Cramahe Township living with George, James and Charles, as well as Sarah A. (KELLY) JOHNSON and Ethel JOHNSON.20
