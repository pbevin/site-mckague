Notes for Mary Victoria HUNTINGTON
Mary was one of 7 children. The couple lived in Walnut Grove MN.444

Brian McKague has traced the HUNTINGTONs back to England in the 1400s, and a HUNTINGTON signed the Declaration of Independence.385

"I found an 'obituary' article entitled 'J.W. McKague's mother succumbs'. To paraphrase, it says she was born in Ontario on 11-02-1837. She moved with her parents to Lodi, Wisconsin in her early teens. Here she married Solon Perry. Five children were born, three boys and two girls. She then married Joseph McKague on June 15th 1871. ( Joe's obit said July) Three boys and two girls were born. Two boys survive, Winn McKague of Farmington and Roy McKague of Pierre South Dakota. She died 3-28-1931 at the age of 93 in Pierre So. Dak."438
