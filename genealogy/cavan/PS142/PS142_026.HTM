Notes for Delbert Deforest McKAGUE
"Delbert's great granddaughter is Barbara Davis. I have not spoke to her yet but she states that she believes her family is related to the McKagues from Missouri."242

"The story is that the father left when he was young, went 'west' and was to send for the family. They heard no more from him so the mother took Delbert (not known if there were other siblings) and went searching for him. She stopped in various places, working until she could afford to go further. Sometime along the way she died and Delbert was taken in by a family by the name of Stone. Delbert eventually ended up in Colorado."775

In 1920, Delbert was renting a place at Grand Ave. and 6th St., 2nd precinct, Del Norte Twp, Rio Grande Co., Colorado.560
Research Notes
NAME: His first name(s) is/are also given as Delbert.559

BIRTH: There are some assumptions here about parentage. The 1875 census shows a Delbert living with William (and presumably his son),559 and he appears to match up with the information we have from other sources about Delbert Deforest. His date and place of birth are given in one source with no parentage given.775 His year and place of birth are confirmed with no parentage given.774 He is shown in the 1875 census as 6, born in Michigan.559 He is shown in the 1920 census as 52, born in Michigan, parents born in Michigan.560 The 1930 census shows him as 66, born in Michigan, parents born in Michigan.776

MARRIAGE: His wife is identified as Lizzie (no last name) with no date or place given in one source.560 The date of his marriage (to an unknown spouse) is approximately confirmed ("first married at age 30").776
