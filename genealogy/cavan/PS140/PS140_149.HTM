Notes for Ruth CAMPBELL
"Ruth was a health and physical education teacher at a Toronto high school. ... Barrie and I had enjoyable visits with her at her home, and she came to Bradford to our McKague family reunion in September, 1983. She had made an interesting scrap book/photo album on the McKague family which she promised to give us some time, as she had no children herself. We mever received it. She gave Barrie a lovely yellow vase and a glass vinegar cruet that had belonged to Emily Jane McKague (nee Giles). Unfortunately, we lost contact with her in the late 1980s. Her late husband's family never contacted us, and so none of us has any idea what happened. As a result of a fall, she had become lame in one leg and may have had to leave her home. There were other family items, along with the album, that we never saw again, and information we will never get."213
