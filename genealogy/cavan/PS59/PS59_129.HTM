"Grandpa did not seem to want to talk about his past or his heritage. Whereas I got lots and lots of detailed information from my Grandmother about her heritage, Grandpa generally refused to talk about his. I do recall him mentioning that he had several half brothers. Names I had made a record of were Carl, Wilbur J., Ora Wilson, and George. A couple of them, Carl and another I don't recall, had moved to Los Angeles in the thirties or forties.
"It may be, Carla, that I am incorrect about Ora Wilson being Grandpa's half brother; memories of those conversations with Grandpa are now over 40 years old. Given that the Ora you refer to was writing letters in 1941, I doubt that Grandpa was referring to a different Ora.
