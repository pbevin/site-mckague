Notes for Minnie Elginetta McKAGUE
Minnie was the first white child born in the Wycollar District and on the original homestead. At the 50th anniversary celebrations of the province of Saskatchewan (1905-1955), held in Marsden on the 1st of July, they put up a cairn to commemorate the event, and Minnie was one of two who did the unveiling, because she was the first child born in the district.205

"We saw a lot of Aunt Minnie as she lived within a reasonable driving distance. She was a loving, mother-earth type with a wonderful laugh." (Sharon Proctor)205
