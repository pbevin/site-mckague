Clipping from Margaret Jane' s Family Bible
"Mr. John Lummis Killed: Simcoe County License Commissioner Victim of Runaway Accident"- (Special despatch to the Globe)
"Barrie, Oct. 29 - John Lummis of Wyebridge died in the Victoria Hospital here today as a result of injuries received in a runaway yesterday. After attending a meeting of the License Commissioners he started last evening to drive home. Some hours later he was found lying unconscious on the roadside at the outskirts of the town, with his head badly crushed and several ribs broken. The deceased was 72 years of age, and was one of the best known Liberals in Centre Simcoe. For over twenty years he had been a member of the Board of License Commissioners for this riding."767
