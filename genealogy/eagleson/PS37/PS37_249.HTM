Notes for Margaret Maria COCHRANE
When Maggie was widowed, she hired a Barnardo boy from England named Harry Double. He sent her an apron of white silk appliqued with coloured silk flowers. It was given to her daughter Florence after WWI as a gift from Paris. "[Florence] longed for more information about Harry Double."49
