Notes for Nettie JACKSON
The Simcoe Land Registry shows Ira Jackson selling the family property in Normandale (or a share of the property), to his sister Nettie Post on 24 October 1914 for $600. This was shortly after the death of their father Daniel. Nettie's husband, John Post, was of the local Post family.80
