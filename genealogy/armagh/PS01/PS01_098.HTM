Notes for Yvonne McKAGUE
Yvonne lived in Toronto ON .2 She studied at, and became a teacher at, the Ontario College of Art. Afterward, she went to Paris to the Academie de la Grande Chaumiere, the Academie Colarossi and the Academy Ranson. In 1930, she went to Vienna to study child art under Cizek and Dynamic Symmetry in Mexico under Emil Bistram. Also, at Cape Cod, she studied under Hans Hofmann. O.S.A. in 1927 and R.C.A. in 1951. She was a founding member (1933) of the Canadian Group of Painters. Her work is in most Canadian galleries.50
