Notes for Samuel KIRKLAND
Sam immigrated to Newtonville, where he appears on the 1856 assessment roll as "carpenter and builder". In 1858, after marrying Elizabeth, he moved to Culross Twp to farm on the eastern edge of Teeswater.4
