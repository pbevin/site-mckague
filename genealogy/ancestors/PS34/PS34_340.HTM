Notes for John RICHARDS
The Richardses originally came from Wales, and one (possibly this one) went to France some time before 1572. He is said to have served in the king's bodyguard, and to have helped the royal family flee the palace during the St. Bartholomew's Eve massacre of the Huguenots on August 23, 1572.111
