The translation of her baptism record reads:
"On this day, 22 May, 1710, has been baptized by me Missionary Priest, undersigned, a slave, of the Panis nation, belonging to Jean Richard, voyageur, age at baptism about fifteen years, and named Marie Jeanne, the Godfather is Michael Beaugis, voyageur, and the Godmother, Madeleine Parent, both have signed the same day."111
