Notes for Malcolm , King of Alba
He acceded to the throne in 943. He was killed in battle by rebels from Moray.153

His great-great-granddaughter (through his son Dubh or Duf) was Shakespeare's Lady Macbeth. Dubh, who was King of Alba 962-966, was killed by his third cousin Culen who then ruled as King of Alba 966 - 971. (Culen in turn was killed by Ryderch, King of Strathclyde, whose daughter he had kidnapped.)153
