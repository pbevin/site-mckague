Notes for Aethelred II, King of England
"Reigned 979-1013 (deposed) and 1014-1016. In the face of Danish raids, he was forced to pay huge tributes (Danegeld) to the enemy. He was driven into exile by Sweyn but returned after [Sweyn's] death. Died during Canute's invasion of England. His tomb was lost when the old St. Paul's was destroyed in the great fire of London."149
