Notes for Henry I, King of England
Henry and Matilda were multiply related with the closest (so far) being that they were half fifth cousins once removed. Henry and Adeliza were multiply related with the closest (so far) being that they were fifth cousins twice removed. Henry and Nest were fifteenth cousins once removed. Henry and Isabel were multiply related with the closest (so far) being that they were second cousins once removed.

"Reigned 1100-1135. Duke of Normandy 1106-1135. His reign is notable for important legal and administrative reforms, and for the final resolution of the investiture controversy. Abroad, he waged several campaigns in order to consolidate and expand his continental possessions. Was so hated by his brothers that they vowed to disinherit him. In 1106 he captured Robert and held him til he died. He proved to be a hard but just ruler. He apparently died from overeating lampreys!"149
