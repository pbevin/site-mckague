Research Notes
BIRTH: Thomas's birthdate is also given as ca 1492.91

GENERAL: There is clearly an error here somewhere. Thomas is shown in the source91 as having two daughters named Margaret, born 25 years apart and dying on the same day (Aug 25, 1612). One of the daughters shown in the source must belong to somebody else, and the death date must belong only to one of them.

It is most probable that the death date is that of the Margaret who married William ALLGAR, as attributing it to the Margaret who married William MARLAR presents big problems with respect to MARLAR's second marriage. I am going to proceed on that assumption. However, I have no way of determining--or even making an educated guess at--which Margaret is really Thomas's daughter.
