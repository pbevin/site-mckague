Notes for Jaime OF ARAGON , King of Aragón
"Like his grandfather, ascended to the crown at the age of five. It was run by a regency until he came of age; his uncle Sanc, Count of Provence, managed the finances and paid off his father's debts. He later became known for leading military campaigns that captured Majorca and the other Balearic Islands, as well as Valencia, from the Moors."149
