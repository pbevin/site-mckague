Notes for Roger DE MORTIMER , Earl of March, Lord of Leix
"English magnate. He led the baronial opposition to Edward II's favourites (1320-22) and was imprisoned before fleeing to France. There he became the lover of Edward's Queen Isabella, with whom he secured Edward's deposition and murder in 1327. He then ruled England in the name of Edward's son Edward III, until the latter caused him to be executed."149
