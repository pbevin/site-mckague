Notes for Iago AP IDWAL , Prince of Gwynned
"[He] was at first excluded from the throne of Gwynedd by Llywelyn ap Seisyll (also Llewellyn ap Sitsyllt) from 1018 to 1023. Iago ap Idwal was Prince of Gwynedd from 1023 to 1039 when he was murdered by Llywelyn ap Seisyll's son Gruffydd ap Llewelyn, who was the only Welsh king to actually rule over the whole of Wales from 1057 until his death in 1063."153
