Notes for Anarawd AP RHODRI MAWR , King of Gwynned
"[He] became King of Gwynedd in 878 on the death of his father. He abandoned an alliance with the Danish Kingdom of York and acknowledged Ælfred the Great as overlord; as did his brothers and other lesser rulers. The precise nature of this overlordship is not known, and there was an attempt to portray this submission as a desire for unity among Christian rulers against the pagan Danes.  However, this recognition by Welsh rulers that that the King of England had claims upon them would be a central fact in the subsequent history of Wales."153
