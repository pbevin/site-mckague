Notes for Samuel WILSON
Samuel's parents were from Northern Ireland.12 He settled on the north half of Lot 15, 6th Concession, Percy Township, Northumberland County. The original crown grant was made to him on May 10, 1841107 for the sum of £56 5s.5 He came with 50¢, which he used to buy an axe. He built a cabin, cut wood and burned it for potash, which he sold to buy grain.5
