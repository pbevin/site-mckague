Notes for Robert DE QUINCY
"Robert de Quincy is referred to in a letter signed by Eliza S. Quincy of Cambridge, Massachusetts and dated June 17, 1844 as the father of Saher de Quincy. The Dictionary of National Biography, (Sidney Lee, editor; London, 1894) suggests that Robert de Quincy may have been an older brother of Saher de Quincy, but allows that he may also have been Saher de Quincy's eldest son!"153
