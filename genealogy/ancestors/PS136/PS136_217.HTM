Notes for Ermengard D'ANJOU
"Ermengard was the sister of Geoffrey 'Martel', Count of Anjou who was born on October 14, 996 and d.s.p. (decessit sine prole, died without issue) on November 14, 1066.  Thus, Ermengard's descendants became the Counts of Anjou."153
