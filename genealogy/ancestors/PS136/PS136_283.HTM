Notes for Aethelwulf , King of England
He reigned 839-856 (abdicated). He was Underking of Kent 825-839 and 856-858. Renowned for his military prowess, he reputedly defeated 350 Viking ships (851). He reduced taxation, endowed the Church, made lay lands inheritable, and provided systems of poor relief.149
