Notes for Diarmait MACMAIL NA MBÓ , King of Chennselaig, King of Leinster, High King of Ireland
"Diarmait was King of Leinster from 1042 to 1052, at which time he became High King of Ireland, a post which he held from 1052 until his death on February 7, 1072. When Diarmait became High King of Ireland in 1052, it appears that he resigned as King of Leinster and his son took over that post."153
