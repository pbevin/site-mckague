Notes for James H. McGREGOR
James never married.2

According to Doris (McKague) Rusk, he was shingling a roof on a very hot day. The others got off the roof and although they begged him to do the same he refused. Evidently he suffered sunstroke and went into a coma.2 He lived for decades in the psychiatric hospital in Whitby ON and died there.11
