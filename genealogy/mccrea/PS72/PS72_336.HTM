Notes for Walter McCREA
"WALTER McCREA ... chose the profession of the law for his life's work and became very prominent in law and politics. He practiced his profession in Chatham, Ontario where he was elected to serve in local offices and finally to became one of the first Dominion Senators. He was then appointed to be the Judge of the old Algoma District and moved to Sault Ste. Marie, Ontario. As Judge, he had to travel over his district, often by boats and canoes, over wilderness trails and for long distances from his home. He served with such distinction that his decisions were never reversed on appeal and he was recognized for his services by Queen Victoria."32
