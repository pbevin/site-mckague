Notes for James McCREA
"JAMES McCREA was born in Stillwater, New York, in 1787 and ... came with his family to Canada after 1793. ... In 1808 he received a grant of land in Montague Township near his family's land title to which he took with his younger brother, ISAAC McCREA. ... He served in the armed forces of Canada during the War of 1812, and was granted a veteran's allowance for his services. ... He lived on his farm nearAlgonquin north of Maitland, Ontario until his death on February 7, 1873."32
