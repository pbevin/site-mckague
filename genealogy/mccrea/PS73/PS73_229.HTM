Notes for Asa WEBSTER
At Asa's birth in 1800, his family was living on the east half of lot 4 on the first concession of Elizabethtown Township. In 1806, they moved to lot 35 on the fifth concession of Augusta Township.
Asa left home about 1820. After his marriage to Clarinda in 1825, the couple lived in the log house on Lot 35. They bought the rear half of Lot 32 (100 acres) on Oct. 2, 1826 for 100 pounds.
About 1834, Asa and Clarinda left Augusta Township and went to Montague Township in Lanark County.107
