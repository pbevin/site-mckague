Notes for James NETTLETON
There are a James NETTLETON and a Samuel NETTLETON shown on the 1871 census for Arnprior, where Samuel Stafford NETTLETON's family is also shown.79 It seems very probable that these are his sons, who are not living in his home, and I have made that assumption here.

In 1871 this family consists of James, Sarah Ann, William and Elizabeth.79 In 1891 it consists of James, Sarah, William, Elizabeth, Samuel, Alice and James.110
