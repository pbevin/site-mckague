Notes for Daniel MIGEON dit DEBRANSSAT dit DELAGAUCHETIERE
Daniel appears twice in the 1681 census. He is shown in Montréal with his family (see his father's card), and also in Québec in what appears to be either a seminary or some sort of residence for the bishop and priests. He is listed there as follows:
Daniel MIGEON, 10.109

At the time of his marriage he is described as "écuyer, lieutenant, aide-major des troupes de ce pays" [squire, lieutenant, ? in the troops of this country].109

At the time of his daughter Marie Therese's marriage, he is described as "écuyer, chevalier de l'ordre militaire de St-Louis, capitaine d'une compagnie de la marine" [squire, knight of the military order of St-Louis, captain of a navy company].109
