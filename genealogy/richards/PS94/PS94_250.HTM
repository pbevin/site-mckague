Marguerite BESNARD, 50, "habitant", Jean DORVAL, 25, Paul DORVAL, 19; Madeleine ?, 22, Augustin ?, 4, Jean ?, 1. These last three people are Marie Madeleine CLOUTIER, wife of Jean [Baptiste], above, and her children Augustin GRAVEL dit BRINDELIERE and Jean Baptiste BOUCHARD dit DORVAL.109
Research Notes
NAME: Her last name is also given as BENART, as BESNARD, and as BESNAR.109

MARRIAGE: Her marriage contract with Cesar was executed on Aug 17, 1647 at an unknown place in PQ before notary Claude Lecoustre. The marriage is registered in Québec Parish, but a note on the entry indicates it was celebrated in Sillery Parish.109
