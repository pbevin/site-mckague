Notes for Simeon J. WINN
Webster and Adeline moved to Sheboygan County, Wisconsin. About 1859, they returned to Canada, staying there for several years. They returned to Sheboygan County and in 1876 they bought eighty acres of wild land in York Township, Clark County.99
