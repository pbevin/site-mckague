Research Notes
NAME: His last name is also given as PAYETTE,49 and as PEYET, PEQUET, and PEYAIT,109 and his dit name as ST-AMOURE and as STE-AMOURE.109

BIRTH: A note on his baptismal certificate reads "born the preceding day".109 His parentage is confirmed with no birthdate or place given.49

BAPTISM: The day of the month is illegible in the register because of damage. It occurs between Sep 5, 1726 and Sep 21, 1726.109
