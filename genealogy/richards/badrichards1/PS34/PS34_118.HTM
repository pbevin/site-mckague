Notes for Agnes PRICE
Obituary, unknown newspaper:
"Agnes Gunter, 71, of 99 Ontario St. died Sunday May 6, 1984 at the Trenton Memorial Hospital. She had been ill for six months.
"Born in Newcastle, Ontario, she was the daughter of the late John and Margaret Price. She was the wife of the late Clifford Gunter. She is the mother of Gene of Wooler and Keith of Trenton.
"She is the sister of Mrs. Earl (Jessie) Parnell of Trenton and the late Douglas and Henry Price. She is survived by eight grandchildren and five great grandchildren.
"Services were held Tuesday May 8, 1984 at 1:30 p.m. at the Weaver Funeral Home, conducted by Rev. Keith Joyce. Interment took place at the Mount Evergreen Cemetery.
"Pallbearers were Terry, Ted and Peter Gunter, Donald Bailey, Robert Perry and James Gillispie."201
