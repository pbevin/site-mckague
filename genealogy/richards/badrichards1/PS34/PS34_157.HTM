Research Notes
NAME: Her first name is also given as Almida.207

BIRTH: Almeda's age at her first marriage is given as 30.214 She may have been a GILES rather than a HOWSER.210

MARRIAGE: Almeda may have remarried, though it doesn't quite match up. See Ontario Archives, marriage registrations MS932-53-4505, Sidney:
"Jonathan Shannon, 16 of Murray, born Murray, bachelor, farmer, son of Able and Elisa Shannon, married Almeda Hennessey, 30, of Murray, born Sidney, widow, daughter of Margaret and John Hennessy. Witnesses; B.F. Edmonds, Frankford. At Frankford, 13 October 1886. Both bride and groom Methodist. Rev. B.V. Edwards. By license."

There is also a reference in Peter Hennessy's book The Hennessys of the Bay of Quinte, p. 56, to her having remarried, to an M. Shannon.125
