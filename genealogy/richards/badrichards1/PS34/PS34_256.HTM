Notes for Urbain TESSIER dit LAVIGNE
Urbain was an immigrant from France.105 According to his son Urbain's baptismal certificate, he was captured by the Iroquois on Mar 4, 1661.109

This family is shown in the 1666 census of Montréal as follows:
Urbain TESSIER LAVIGNE, 42, "habitant", Marie ARCHAMBAUT, 30, Paul, 15, Laurent, 11, Louise, 9, Agnes, 7, Urbain, 4, Jean, 3, Claude, 7 wks (children's last name shown as TESSIER); Etienne Gentes, 18, "domestique, engagé" [servant, ?], Jacques Hurtaut, 25, "domestique, engagé" [servant, ?].109

The family is shown in the 1667 census of Montréal as follows:
Urbain TESSIER, 40, "habitant", Marie ARCHAMBAULT, 33, Paul Laurent, 15, Laurent, 11, Agnes, 8, Urbain, 6, Jean, 4, Claude, 17 mo, Louise, 9; Etienne Launay, 21, "domestique" [servant], ? Lamondor, 22, "domestique" [servant].109

The family is shown in the 1681 census of Montréal as follows:
Urbain TESSIER, 55, "habitant", Marie ARCHAMBAULT, 46, Paul, 30, Laurent, 25, Urbain, 20, Jean, 18, Claude, 16, Baptiste, 10, Jacques, 6, Ignace, 4, Nicolas, 2, Petronille, 12.109
