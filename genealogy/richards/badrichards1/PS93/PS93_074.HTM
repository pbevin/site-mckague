Notes for Jean Baptiste PRUDHOMME dit SANSCARTIER
Jean Baptiste came from the parish of Bauchan, diocese of Coutances in Normandy. At his marriage, he was "sergent des troupes de la compagnie de M. Lafreniere" [sergeant of troops in M. Lafreniere's company]. At his third child's baptism in 1744, he was "sergent dans les troupes, en garnison au Fort St-Frederic" [sergeant in the troops, in the garrison at Fort St-Frederic]. At his fifth child's baptism in 1749 he was "sergent des troupes du détachement de la marine" [sergeant of the troops of the navy detachment].109
