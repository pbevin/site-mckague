Research Notes
NAME: Her first name(s) is are also given as Marguerite,109 112 and her last name as LUISSIER, as LUSIER, as LUCIER, as LUISSIE, as LUSSIE, as LEUSSIE, as LHUYSSIER, and as LHUSSIER.109

BIRTH: Her date of birth is also given as Aug 3, 1683 with no parentage given and the place given as Varennes.112 (Note that the parents' residence at the time was Varennes.)109 Given that she was baptized in Pointe-aux-Trembles (see below), the place of birth is very dubious.

BAPTISM: The baptism is registered in Boucherville, but with a note that it occurred in Pointe-aux-Trembles.109 The place of baptism is confirmed with the date given as Aug 3, 1683.112
