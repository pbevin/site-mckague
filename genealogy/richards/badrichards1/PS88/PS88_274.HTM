Notes for Louis Thomas DEJONQUAIRE dit CHAUBERT
At his first child's baptism he is described as "écuyer, enseigne d'une compagnie de la marine" [squire, ensign of a company of the navy]. By the time of his son Louis Romain's baptism, he is "écuyer, lieutenant d'une compagnie du détachement de la marine" [squire, lieutenant of a company of the navy detachment].109
Research Notes
NAME: His first name(s) is/are also given as Thomas, and his last name as DEJONQUIERS, as DEJONCAIRE and as DEIONCAIRE.109

MARRIAGE: On Jun 14, 1700 at an undetermined place in PQ he executed a marriage contract with Marie Louise MAGNAN dite LESPERANCE, daughter of Jean MAGNAN dit LESPERANCE and Marie Marguerite MOITIE, before notary Jean Baptiste POTTIER.109
