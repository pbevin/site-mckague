Obituary, Kingston Whig-Standard, Friday, May 28, 1982:
"Wemp, David Russell--At the Lennox and Addington County Hospital on Friday, May 28, 1982, David Russell in his 79th year, beloved husband of Helen Hagerman, dear son of the late Benjamin Wemp and Ellen Bray, survived by several nieces and nephews, predeceased by brothers William, Seaver and Edward. Resting at the Wannamaker-Tierney Funeral Home, Napanee. Friends and relatives will be received Fri 7-9 pm. Funeral service will be held in the Chapel at 1:30 pm on Sat., May 29, 1982. Interment at Sandhurst Cemetery. Donations to the Lennox and Addington County Hospital would be appreciated in lieu of flowers."188
