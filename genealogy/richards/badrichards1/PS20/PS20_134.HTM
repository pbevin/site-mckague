"McGinnis Mavis P. At Kingston General Hospital on Thursday December 8th 1994, Mavis Pauline McGinn, wife of the late Delbert McGinness, dear mother of Harold and wife Jean, Brighton; Jean and Jim Miller, RR#2, Napanee; Judy Smith, Belleville, and Cindy McGinness, Napanee; dearly remembered by many grandchildren and one great grandchild, loving daughter of Marion McGinn, Kingston; sadly missed by brothers Arthur and John McGinn, Amherst Island and Donald McGinn, Coburg [sic]; predeceased by her son Robert Delbert McGinness and by sisters Leona, Annie and June and brother Bryce.
