Kingston Whig-Standard, Feb 15, 1950 (summarized by Eric Bowler):
Stella:--25th wedding anniversary of Edward Wemp and Sarah Glenn; children listed as Christena (Mrs. Melville Filson), Grace, Nina and Hugh, all residing on the island, and Benjamin, Kingston. One grandchild, Hugh Filson.192

Obituary, Kingston Whig-Standard, Jan 30, 1961:
"Wemp, Sarah Jane:--Suddenly at her late residence, Stella, on Sat. Jan. 29, 1961. Sarah Jane Glenn, beloved wife of Edward Wemp; dear mother of Mrs. Melville Filson (Christina), Stella; Mrs. Frank Bailey (Margie), Toronto; Mrs. Eric Dailey (Grace), Toronto; Mrs. William Churchill (Nina), Kingston; Hugh, Centreville; Ben, Amherstview; and dear sister of Mrs. Ross Filson (Tena), Stella, and Alex Glenn, Stella. Resting at her late residence for funeral services in St. Alban's Church on Jan 31 at 2 pm. Interment Glenwood cemetery. Rev. W. Williston officiating."191
