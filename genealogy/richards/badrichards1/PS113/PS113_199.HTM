Notes for Antoine Andre GAUTHIER dit LANDREVILLE
Antoine and Sophie are fourth cousins.

Marquette Mining Journal, Marquette, Michigan, July 1907:
"Were Married 57 Years
"Death of Antoine Gauthier, who had lived in Marquette many years
"The funeral of Antoine Gauthier, who died at the age of eighty four from cancer of the face at his home, 206 Rock St. Thursday night, will be held Monday morning. Services will be held at the French Catholic Church and the interment will be in the Holy Cross Cemetery.
"Mr. Gauthier, who was widely known, came to Marquette, Marquette Co. MI US from Canada thirty eight years ago. Until a year ago when the affliction which caused his death appeared, he retained his vigor, although then an aged man. For the past four months he had been confined to his bed. Seven years ago he celebrated his golden wedding anniversary and his wife, who with his eight children who survive him, is seventy four years old. The surviving children are: Mrs. Julia Santamore, Mrs. Josephine Durand, Joseph, Louis, Antoine Jr., Henry, Dan, and Sam Gauthier."270
