Notes for Walter Dale HOLTSCLAW
"He was a self made man. If anything needed to be done, he did it. His favorite term to call the children was 'Kid'. He loved his wife Camilla Mary White, who preceded him in death. He worked as a self employed Heating and Air Conditioning Maintenance Man. He would not let any of his customers be cold in the winter and worked hard to keep them cool in the summer. He dug his own well for water. He also put in his cesspool. He sold part of his land to Don Windes. He like to read books and watch John Wayne movies. He was a very kind hearted man who loved his family very much. He was a World War II veteran."116
