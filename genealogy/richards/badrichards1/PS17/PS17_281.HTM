Notes for Hazelton SPENCER
Hazelton lived in Fredericksburgh.115 He served as a Lieutenant with the 2nd Battalion, K.R.R., New York. He was Commandant of Fort Frontenac from 1799 to 1803. He established a home on the Bay Shore, about two miles west of Conway. He was appointed Lieutenant of Lennox County, and served in the first Parliament of Ontario in 1792.105
