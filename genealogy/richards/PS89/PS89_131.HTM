Notes for Marc Antoine DESJARDINS dit DERUPALLEY dit GONNEVILLE
A note on his burial entry reads (in translation):
"He drowned about five weeks ago and was found near Tremblay and later brought to Longueuil, where his his widow and her sister came with many friends, who assisted with the funeral procession. Monsieur Meinard, soldier, carried the cross. Madame de Longueuil provided the linen."109
