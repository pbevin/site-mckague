Research Notes
NAME: Her last name is also given as SPENSER,114 and her first name as Dianah.117

DEATH: She is also said to have died (after giving birth to twins, one of whom died at six months) at age 31 or 34, which would be between 1792 and 1796.105 The birthdate we have for William Augustus and (probably) Owen would make that age wrong, unless her birthdate is erroneous. A further complication is that we are told her daughter Mary (Polly) took over the household at age 13,105 which would appear to be in about 1795.
