Research Notes
BIRTH: Samuel is shown in the 1851 census as 25,14 and in the 1871 census as 44.12 His age at death is given as 86.9

MARRIAGE: The date of Samuel's marriage to Emily is given as Aug 1, 1839, but this appears to be a typo for 1849.11 The marriage is confirmed with no date or place given.14
