Notes for Allen Bruce CURTIS
Bruce was a spoiled boy and got into a lot of trouble both in Canada and the States--women and money problems. He had been bailed out a number of times. Eventually his siblings refused to bail him out any more. He committed suicide; the family brought his body back to Warkworth for burial.223
