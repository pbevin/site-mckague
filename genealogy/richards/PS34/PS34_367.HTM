Notes for Ransom CURTIS
In 1865 Ransom was living on Con. 2, Lot 17 with his father John Curtis.26

In 1871 he was living in Percy Twp with Lizzie and Minnie.221

In 1881 he was living in Percy Twp with Elizabeth, Minnie, Hiram, Lulu, Lillian and a servant, Mary DeFrame, 18. He was listed in the provincial census as an accountant.38

In 1891 he was living in Percy Twp with Elizabeth, Minnie, Hiram, Lulu, Lillian, Carrie, Bruce and his sister Sally.39

In his will Ransom left his real property to Hiram and Bruce. He left all personal belongings and monies to his wife Elizabeth, his gold watch to his son Hiram and the piano to his four daughters, to go to Clara after she attained the age of 17.26
