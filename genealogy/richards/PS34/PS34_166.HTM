Notes for Catherine MURPHY
Obituary, Belleville Intelligencer, Oct. 17, 1933:
"Mrs. C.M. Hennessy
"Toronto, Oct. 17 - Mrs. Catherine M. Hennessy, widow of W.H. Hennessy, formerly of Trenton, died Wednesday in her 76th year. Most of her life was spent in Prince Edward County. She lived in Toronto for the past ten years and was active in St. Anthony's R.C. Church, the Catholic Women's League and Christian Mother's Society. Surviving are three daughters, Mrs. R. Marshall, Washago; Mrs. Douglas Webster, Toronto and Miss Kathleen Hennessy at home; four sons, John and Dan, Trenton and William and George Hennessy, Toronto and seven grandchildren."215
