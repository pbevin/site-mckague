Notes for Martin Howard PETERSON
Martin was a fruit exporter in Colborne in 1891. By 1897, the Toronto City Directory shows M.H. Peterson and Company (Martin H. and Alexander E.W. Peterson, apple exporters, 25 Church St.). Both have home addresses in Toronto. By 1907, the only identifiable address is for Albert E.W. Peterson, apple broker. The street listing lists Alexander E.W. at this address--Albert may be Alexander's son. The last city directory listing for the Petersons is a 1910 listing for Albert E.W.
