Notes for Willemijna VAN WILL
Willemijna did not marry, and was still alive in 1778, as shown by the will of Aegidius (Dilis) van Will, miller in Beek en Donk (IVa) on 07-10-1778: "Furthermore he declared his aforementioned children and heirs responsible for the alimentation of Anna Maria and Wilhelmina van Will, for whom the hereafter appointed executors will have to take care the paying of interest from a fortune of five hundred guilders to the executor Eijnde bequeathed, as well as the rental income of a certain house and garden, situated on the Wiel in Helmont, further will all of testator's heirs have to contribute alimentation to the aforementioned of six guilders per year, while after the death of mentioned indigent persons a fortune of five hundred guilders will be divided between the total heirs and they will be discharged from the mentioned contribution."7
