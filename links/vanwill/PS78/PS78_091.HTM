Notes for Aegidius VAN WILL
Gillis moved in 1690 to Helmond. On May 2, 1690 the mayor of Helmond signed a contract about the letting of the mills in Helmond for the period of six years (Huisarchief Helmond Inv. 120, page 32). After his death in 1694, his wife paid the rent for the mills for the remaining years.7
Research Notes
NAME: His first name is also given as Gillis and Jilis, and his last name as VAN WIL and VAN WIJL.7

CHILDREN: It is likely that more children were born in Helmond between 1690 and 1694, because on May 10, 1694 a child of Jilis van Will, named Willemijn, died in Helmond.7
