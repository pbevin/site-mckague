More Links


This page includes links to other pages that we've enjoyed or found useful. It's not meant to be exhaustive, just a selection of a few places we really liked. We hope they'll help you. (If you want exhaustive, go to Cyndi's List below.)

DO A GOOD DEED--FOR FREE

You can do a kindness for nothing except a few minutes of your time. You click; the page's sponsors pay. The sites are linked to each other, so you only need to bookmark one of them.

You can make a basic donation once a day by clicking on the main "Donation" button. Pick one, two or more. Or rotate them and help different causes on different days.

The Hunger Site. Donate food to Mercy Corps (international aid) and America's Second Harvest (US aid).

The Rainforest Site. Help preserve the world's rapidly shrinking rainforest by bringing a few square feet of it under protection.

The Breast Cancer Site. You can help fund mammograms for women in the US who can't afford them.

The Child Health Site. Save children's lives and improve their health in a number of ways (described on the site).

The Animal Rescue Site. Help provide food for abandoned or abused animals.

DON'T BE A SUCKER

Inboxer Rebellion. Is that child REALLY missing? Is The Gap REALLY giving away money? Check it out here before you forward the good or bad news on. (We found this out the hard way.)

CANADIAN GENEALOGY

Ontario GenWeb. Includes pages of genealogical, historical and general information about every county and district in Ontario. And you can get from there to Canada GenWeb and beyond to the rest of the world.

Programme de recherche en démographie historique. Everything there is to know about pre-1800 Québec genealogy. You have to pay to do anything beyond the most superficial, but it's worth every penny. This is the English version, from which you can just click to the French one if you prefer it.
