Obituary:
"FARAGHER - The death occurred on Wednesday, June 29th, 1977 of Mr. Walter Faragher of 41 Federal Street, Kirkland Lake. He was 26 years of age. He is survived by his parents Mr. and Mrs. Clifford Faragher and one brother Jim and one sister Anita (Mrs. J. Haine) one nephew Christopher Haine all of Kirkland Lake. Resting at the Symington Funeral Home on Thursday, June 30, 1977 from 7 to 9 p.m. until time for the funeral in the chapel on Friday, July 1, 1977 at 11 a.m. Rev. Sorensen officiating. Interment will follow in the Kirkland Lake cemetery. In memoriam, contributions to the Canadian Mental Health Association will be gratefully acknowledged."106
