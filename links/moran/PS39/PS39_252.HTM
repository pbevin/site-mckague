Notes for Tancrède Pierre DUBEAU
Obituary, 1965:
"TANCREDE DUBEAU
"Funeral service was held February 1st, in the Assumption Church, Kirkland Lake, for Tancrede Pierre Dubeau, who died January 30th in Kirkland Lake District Hospital, at the age of 73. Rev. Father Lafond conducted the service, and interment was made in Kirkland Lake cemetery.
"A former resident of Cochrane, Mr. Dubeau was born in St. Gabriel de Brandon, Que. He had lived for the past 20 years in Kirkland Lake.
"Mr. Dubeau was predeceased by his wife in 1952. He is survived by 10 daughters and two sons: Lucille (Mrs. Lionel Barrette), Cochrane; Jeanne (Mrs. W. Cook), Connaught; Yolande (Mrs. Aurel Desforge) and Aline (Mrs. Carm Lacroix) both of Hull, Que.; Fleurette (Mrs. Jeff Gauthier), Hearst; Marielle (Mrs. Severe Chartier) and Rachel (Mrs. Lariviere) both of Timmins; Anita (Mrs. Roy Walker), Sault Ste Marie; Annette (Mrs. Clifford Faragher) and Cecile (Mrs. Clifford Lloyd) both of Kirkland Lake; Ronaldo of Toronto and Donald of Latchford. There are 40 grandchildren and one great-grandchild. Also surviving are two sisters and one brother, Mrs. Marie Mondor of Toronto, Eugenie (Mrs. Rodolphe Lefebvre) of Montreal, and Maxime Dubeau of Drummondville, Que."9
