Notes for Raynald LARIVIERE
Obituary (translated from original French):
"LARIVIERE - At Warren, Michigan, Mr. Raynald Larivière died in an accident on June 6 at the age of 33. Born in Timmins, he had lived in Warren for 10 years. He leaves to mourn his death his wife Rita, one daughter, Michèle, three sons, Robert, Albert and Raynald; his parents, Mr. and Mrs. Réné Larivière of Timmins; four brothers: Richard, Lionel, Claude and Marc, all of Timmins; two sisters: Mrs. G. Eckert (Gisèle) and Anne, of Timmins. Viewing was at the Guénette funeral home; the service was held at Notre-Dame-de-Lourdes Church on Tuesday. Interment in the Catholic cemetery."59
