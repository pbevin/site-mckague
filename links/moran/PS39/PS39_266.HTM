Obituary, 1986:
"LLOYD - the death occurred at the Kirkland and District Hospital on Sunday, July 13, 1986 of Mrs. Cecile Lloyd of 70 Fourth Street, Kirkland Lake at the age of 56 years. Born in Cochrane, she had resided in Kirkland Lake since 1962 and was a member of the Holy Name Church. She is survived by her husband Clifford at home, her daughter Debbie (Mrs. Patrick Rody) and son Terry (and wife Karen) all of Elliott Lake, 2 grandchildren Alysha and Travis, her brothers and sisters Mrs. Lucille Barrette of Cochrane, Jean (Mrs. Buzz Cook) of South Porcupine, Yolande (Mrs. Aurel Desforges) of Hull, Mrs. Fleurette Gauthier of Kirkland Lake, Anita (Mrs. Roy Walker) of Sault Ste. Marie, Annette (Mrs. Clifford Faragher) of Kirkland Lake, Mrs. Rachel Lariviere of Timmins, Aline (Mrs. Carmen Lacroix) of Almer, Quebec and Donald Dubeau of Latchford. Resting at the Symington and Monette Funeral Home on Wednesday, July 16, 1968 from 2 to 4 p.m. and 7 to 9 p.m., until the time for the Mass at the Church of the Holy Name on Thursday at 11 a.m. Interment to follow in the Kirkland Lake Cemetery. In Memoriam contributions to the Ontrio [sic] Heart Stroke [sic] foundation will be gratefully acknowledged."48
